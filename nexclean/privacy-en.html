Privacy Policy

中文版

NexClean (hereinafter referred to as "this App") is a photo management tool developed by an individual developer. This App respects and protects the privacy rights of all users.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Local Photo Data

  • This App accesses photos and videos in your device's photo library
  • All photo analysis (including similar photo detection, blur detection, categorization, etc.) is performed locally on your device
  • No photos or videos are transmitted to servers or third parties

1.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • This App does not collect any personally identifiable information
  • This App does not collect device information or usage data
  • This App does not contain any third-party analytics SDKs

2. How We Use Information

This App uses photo data solely for the following purposes:

  • Identifying similar photos, screenshots, blurry images, etc.
  • Organizing and displaying photos by time, category, etc.
  • Helping users manage and free up device storage space

All data processing is performed locally on the device and is not uploaded to any server.

3. Information Sharing

This App does not share any user data with third parties.

4. Data Security

Since all data processing in this App is performed locally on the device, there are no risks associated with data transmission or cloud storage.

5. Permissions

5.1 Photo Library Permission

  • This App requires access to your photo library to provide photo management functionality
  • You can revoke this permission at any time in system settings
  • After revoking permission, this App will not be functional

6. Data Retention

This App does not store any user data on developer servers. All data generated within the App (such as marking for deletion or keeping) is stored only locally on the user's device.

7. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for children under 13 years of age and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Privacy Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Updated policies will be published in the App or on the App Store product page.
